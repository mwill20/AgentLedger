# AgentLedger v0.1.0 - Legal Scope Note

**Status:** POC - not a production or commercial deployment
**Date:** April 2026

---

## What AgentLedger Is

AgentLedger is a proof-of-concept software implementation of trust and discovery
infrastructure for autonomous AI agents. It is a reference architecture and
working codebase demonstrating how trust, context, workflow, and liability evidence
could be structured for the agentic web.

---

## What AgentLedger Is Not

AgentLedger v0.1.0 explicitly does not provide, constitute, or offer:

**Legal services:**
AgentLedger's attribution determinations are computed evidence outputs, not legal
rulings. No output of this software constitutes legal advice, a binding legal
determination, or an enforceable liability finding.

**Insurance products:**
AgentLedger's compliance exports and quality scores are reference data. No output
constitutes an insurance policy, coverage determination, or underwriting decision.
Any insurance product built on AgentLedger data requires a licensed insurer.

**Financial services:**
AgentLedger does not process payments, hold funds in escrow, or execute financial
transactions. Smart contract escrow integration is deferred pending Layer 3 blockchain
deployment.

**Regulatory compliance certification:**
EU AI Act, HIPAA, and SEC export formats are structured reference outputs. They are
not certified compliance reports. Actual regulatory compliance requires qualified
legal and compliance review.

---

## Scope of This Release

This is a proof-of-concept release. It is not deployed to production. It is not
serving real users. It is not processing real agent transactions. It demonstrates
what the architecture can do.
